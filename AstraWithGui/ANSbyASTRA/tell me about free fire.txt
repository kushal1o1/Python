Garena Free Fire is a battle royale mobile game developed by 111 Dots Studio and published by Garena. It was released for Android and iOS devices. It is a survival shooter game where players battle against each other to be the lone survivor. The objective is to defeat all other players using weapons and tactics. Players can choose between various playable characters, each with their own abilities and perks. Additionally, they can customize the weapons and gear for each character. Matches are fast-paced and typically last around 10 minutes.
